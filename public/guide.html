Step-By-Step Guide

1. Install the Kiwi Browser from the Google Play Store and launch it.

2. Open search and go to: Download Extension

3. Download the file.

4. Open Extensions from the menu.

5. Enable Developer Mode and add the extension.

6. Select the downloaded zip file.

7. Activate C3C fbstate.

8. Login to Facebook.

9. Open C3C fbstate from menu.

10. Click Copy Clipboard.

11. Paste cookies at Bot Website

12. Accept terms and submit.

13. Type ai to verify bot is working.

Note

• Unlock account if login fails.

• Don’t login elsewhere.

• Don’t change password while active.

• We are not responsible for bans or locks.

• Logout (not delete appstate) to register another bot.
